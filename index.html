Commission

Commission

It's easy to order a portrait,

start with a good clear photo.

Send Your Photos Email me your photos to get started. I can combine multiple photos to create a single portrait, bringing together people or animals into one composition.

Draft Composition I'll create a digital composition to show the arrangement at your chosen size. Once you approve the draft, I'll begin working on the final portrait.

Select Your Style and Size Choose the portrait style: watercolor, graphite pencil, or modern sketch. Popular sizes include Standard (21x30cm), Large (25x30cm), and Extra Large (30x40cm), but custom sizes are also available.

Completion and Payment Upon completion, I'll send you a scan of the finished portrait for feedback. Minor adjustments can be made until you are fully satisfied. Payment is arranged via PayPal after your approval.
